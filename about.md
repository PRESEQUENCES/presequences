At the very least, it provides a bit more room to write about the things I do — certainly more than I can usually fit into the description fields on [Instagram](https://www.instagram.com/laspalmas7/){:target="_blank"} or [YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/@laspalmas7){:target="_blank"}.

## What's a presequence anyway?

In [linguistics](https://glossary.sil.org/term/presequence){:target="_blank"}, a presequence is the opening part of a conversation, often taking the form of a question.
